Klymet Control Insulated Glass Units provides the ultimate in climate insulation and solar control, substantially reducing heating & cooling costs and enhancing overall occupancy comfort.

Klymet Control units can incorporate combinations of processed clear, tinted, coloured and Low E glass.

Klymet Control IGU’s can offer you energy efficiency, safety, security, great aesthetics, UV protection, increased fire resistance and noise control.

Klymet Control may be filled with Argon gas and can incorporate Klymet Shield Low E glass as well as other High Performance products for superior comfort and performance.

Klymet Shield Low -E glass is designed to improve thermal insulation in both summer and winter.

Klymet Shield Low E has a thin metallic coating on the glass that reflects thermal radiation and inhibits emission, reducing heat transfer through the glass. Low E is built to deflect radiation not absorb it, thus resulting in a lower U-value.

The lower the U-value the better the performance.

Klymet Shield Low E is available in clear and tinted, laminated and toughened versions.
