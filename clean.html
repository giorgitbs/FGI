Nanoclear is a highly advanced finishing process using Nanotechnology to apply a protective, invisible coating which provides long lasting clarity.

Nanoclear coated glass is easier to clean and cleaning time is dramatically reduced with up to 90% less cleaning time required.

Nanoclear coated glass is ideal for glass balustrades & pool fencing, shower screens and kitchen splashbacks, exterior windows and building facades.

The future is clear.

PROTECTS: Durable, anti-stick coating for long lasting protection.
REPELS: Water, dirt, oil, lime, calcium & mineral repellent.
RESISTS: UV radiation, chemical & abrasion resistant.

Requires minimal cleaning to help make your life easier and SAVES water to help the environment.
