FGI Clear Float Glass is a high quality, visually colourless and distortion free flat surface, providing high light transmission (daylight) and clarity.

FGI tinted glass absorbs and re-radiates solar energy reducing heat, providing cost efficient climate control. Tinted Float also reduces sun glare and is aesthetically appealing.

FGI Tinted Float is available in the most commonly used colours: Grey, Green, Bronze & Blue. Many of these colours are now available in a range of depth of colour (light to dark) and in High Performance options.

FGI Reflective Glass achieves even greater solar control than standard tinted float glass by more effectively absorbing and reflecting a major percentage of the sun's direct energy.

FGI currently provides the following 'hard coat' reflective glass products in the below colours: Clear, Neutral, Green, Grey, Bronze and Blue.

FGI Low Iron Float Glass is manufactured in the same manner as ordinary float glass with a large percentage (approximately 90%) of the iron content removed. This process lessens the inherent green tinge evident in normal standard float glass.
